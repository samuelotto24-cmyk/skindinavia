## Part 1: The Website

**skindinavia.vercel.app**

Your current site explains what Skindinavia is. This site makes people feel why they need it.

The first thing a visitor sees isn't a product spec — it's the question: **"Your makeup is gone by 3 PM. Ours isn't."** That hooks someone who's never heard of setting spray. Then we show real faces, real moments — weddings, long days, oily skin — so she sees herself in the product before she ever sees a price.

### What Makes This Different

- **The comparison table** puts Skindinavia next to Urban Decay, Charlotte Tilbury, and drugstore brands — and you win every row. Competitors are literally grayed out.
- **The animated science section** explains your patented cooling technology (Mist → Cool → Lock) in a way no competitor's site does — it plays on a loop with a custom SVG illustration.
- **The founder story** tells the UD split narrative with the real brand lifestyle photo and the line: *"When they reformulated, we didn't. The original is still here."*
- **The 16-hour timeline** maps your wear claim to real life moments (morning → gym → dinner → last call). No other setting spray brand has this.
- **Every product is clickable from anywhere** — a floating Quick Shop bar follows you as you scroll. The search overlay works. The cart and checkout are fully functional.

### Technical Upgrade

| | Current Site | New Site |
|---|---|---|
| Platform | WordPress + BigCommerce | Next.js 16 on Vercel |
| Speed | Slow (200KB+ inline CSS, plugin bloat) | Fast (static generation, edge CDN) |
| Security | WordPress vulnerabilities | Zero attack surface (static + serverless) |
| SEO | Basic | Optimized metadata, semantic HTML, fast Core Web Vitals |
| Animations | None | Custom GSAP + Framer Motion (3D tilt, text reveal, parallax) |
| Search | None | Live search across products, blog, artists |
| Mobile | Basic responsive | Mobile-first with optimized touch interactions |
| Downtime to switch | Zero | Connects to existing BigCommerce backend |

## Part 2: The Brand Hub

**skindinavia.vercel.app/hub**

Right now your biggest growth channel is Instagram — and you're flying blind. The Brand Hub puts everything in one place so you stop guessing and start targeting.

### Dashboard
- Revenue at a glance: monthly total, orders today, average order value, conversion rate
- Top products ranked by units sold and revenue
- Recent orders with real-time status

### Weekly Recap (Emailed Every Monday)
Every Monday at 9:00 AM, a recap lands in your inbox with:
- **What's Working** — which products are trending, which discount codes are converting, what's driving growth
- **This Week's Action Plan** — 3 specific, numbered things to do that week based on your real data
- Example: *"Launch the Summer Proof Kit this week. Email the Urban Decay switchers — they convert at 2.4x your average. Extend SPRING15 through April — it's generating $27 per use."*

### Instagram Analytics (Connected to Your Account)
- **218K followers** tracked with growth velocity
- **Engagement rate, reach, profile visits** — all real-time
- **Top performing posts** with actual photos and metrics (views, saves, shares)
- **Audience insights** — age, gender, location, best times to post
- **Follower growth chart** showing the Goldman IG post spike and sustained momentum

### AI Advisor
- Analyzes your real sales and Instagram data
- Generates actionable recommendations: bundle ideas, email campaigns, competitor analysis, social captions
- Type a question → get a Skindinavia-specific answer, not generic advice

### Marketing Campaigns
- Track email, social, and paid ad performance in one view
- Discount code tracker with revenue attribution
- Email subscriber growth with sparkline

### Product Management
- Full catalog with stock levels (green/yellow/red indicators)
- Edit products directly from the hub
- Inventory alerts when stock is low

### The Pitch
You're not logging into Shopify for sales, Hootsuite for social, Klaviyo for email, and Google Sheets for everything else. **It's all in one place, and the AI connects the dots for you.**
